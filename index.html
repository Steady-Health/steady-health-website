What Steady does

Six engines.
One clear picture.

Health Confidence Score

A single 0–100 score built from 6+ biometric signals, weighted against your personal 90-day baseline. Know how your body is actually doing today.

Circadian Schedule

Know exactly when to do deep work, exercise, and wind down — from the two-process model adapted to your actual sleep patterns, not generic advice.

Illness Radar

Detects deviations from your baseline that match patterns of oncoming illness — often 24–48 hours before you feel symptoms.

Strain & Recovery

Banister TRIMP-based strain scoring from every workout. Recovery from HRV, sleep quality, and resting HR trends. Know when to push and when to rest.

Sleep Intelligence

Sleep score, debt tracking, stage analysis, consistency scoring, and environment monitoring. Find out exactly why you wake up tired.
